As consumers, cross-border commuters or information seekers, the inhabitants of the Euregio Rhine-Waal are increasingly confronted with questions and problems, the origins of which can often be traced back to differences in European legislation or differences in national legislation. The Euregio Rhine-Waal therefore works together with the Office for German Affairs of the SVB, Deutsche Rentenversicherung, UWV, Agentur für Arbeit, the Tax and Customs Administration, the Finanzamt and trade unions in the GrensInfoPunt Euregio Rhine-Waal .

Subsidie advies

Interested parties can contact the office of the Euregio Rhine-Waal for advice on the subsidisation of projects within the framework of INTERREG Germany-Netherlands and of cross-border activities in the field of society, sport and culture . 

Qualified employees inform potential applicants about the subsidy possibilities in the Euregio Rhine-Waal, the subsidy programmes and the contact persons for these programmes, as well as about the subsidy priorities and current project initiatives.

Belangenbeharteging

The Euregio Rhine-Waal is seen as an advocate for the entire border region. We advocate for the interests of the border region with Dutch, German and European institutions and bodies, in order to sustainably improve the cross-border accessibility of the existing supply. The advocacy takes place in all socially relevant areas, from economy and infrastructure to education and culture.
